About Me

As a former bricklayer turned Environmental Engineer, I've cemented my expertise with a Master's in Data Science, forging a unique path that intersects sustainability with data-driven innovation. I'm passionate about delivering customer-centric solutions through agile development, always aiming to create real value at the intersection of technology and eco-conscious practices.

In both my professional endeavors and high-altitude pursuits, I thrive on the challenge, camaraderie, and the continuous quest for peak performance.
